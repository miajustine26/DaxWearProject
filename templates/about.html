About DaxWear


Who we are. What we are. Where we come from.





The Visionaries


DaxWear was started by Carl and Marissa Generoso. They always dreamed of starting up their own clothing business and when they named their son Dax, they knew that the vision for their company was completed. That's how DaxWear got its name. Marissa designs most of the clothing; she has loved art from a young age and loves bringing her artistic visions to life through clothing. Carl works on most of the marketing and branding for Daxwear. Together they are a great team and have created a stellar business.
